Reporting Guidelines

Follow these tips for effective issue reporting

Best Practices

  • Be specific about the location (landmarks, nearby shops)
  • Provide accurate spot type classification
  • Estimate the severity of the problem
  • Add clear photos/videos when possible

Response Time

Our team aims to respond to reports within 24-48 hours. Urgent issues are prioritized based on severity and potential impact.

Priority Levels

Reports are categorized by urgency:

  • Low: Cosmetic issues, minor inconveniences
  • Medium: Functional problems affecting some users
  • High: Safety hazards, critical infrastructure failures
  • Urgent: Immediate danger, widespread impact
