Visakhapatnam Steel Plant

India's first shore-based integrated steel plant with a vision for sustainable growth

Zero Challenges

Visakhapatnam Steel Plant (VSP), also known as RINL (Rashtriya Ispat Nigam Limited), is a key steel producer in India. It's the first shore-based integrated steel plant in the country, with a capacity of 7.3 million tonnes per annum. VSP is a Navaratna Public Sector Undertaking (PSU) under the Ministry of Steel.

Despite facing challenges, VSP has a strategic plan for revival and expansion. Our commitment to excellence and innovation drives us to overcome obstacles and continue serving the nation's infrastructure needs.

Key Features:

  • State-of-the-art manufacturing facilities
  • Environmentally conscious production methods
  • Strong focus on quality and safety
  • Continuous technological upgrades
  • Significant contributor to India's economic growth
